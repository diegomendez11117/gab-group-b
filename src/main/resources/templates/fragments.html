WISTL

The objective of this content is to be able to help the entire immigrant community such as ally and refugees, providing them with all the necessary tools to build a future. Tools such as your personal identification, education, the search for legal representation in immigration processes, among others.

Being able to find your way in a new city or in a new country is always difficult for those who do not know the culture, the language, the customs and the laws. We hope that you can find everything you need to start a new life.
